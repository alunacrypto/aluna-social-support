What is Aluna Social?

Aluna Social is a Social Network and Tradding Platform which allows you to manage all your crypto exchange accounts from one place, the platform also provides a chat bot to interact with your accounts and receive ( push ) notifications and allow you to share trades and ideas with other.

The platform also houses social trading tools ( aka "copy trade", "shadow trading" ) and ability to browse and create public and private "signals".
